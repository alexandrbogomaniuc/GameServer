HOW TO PLAY
EACH SHOT GUARANTEED TO HIT If the targeted enemy is destroyed before your shot reaches it, the value of the shot is returned to you. In the case that the fired shot does not strike your target, the turret shot will bounce off the edges of the screen until it hits another enemy. You can only have a maximum of 10 active turret shots at any given time.
EACH SHOT HAS A CHANCE TO WIN Any shot fired has a chance to destroy the enemy it hits! If any shots remain in the air at the end of the round without having hit any target, your wager will be returned to you. You cannot change your bet while you have active shots in flight.

ENEMY ENTOURAGE
This enemy will appear on screen surrounded by a number of other smaller enemies circling around it.
If you destroy all the circling enemies, the central enemy becomes vulnerable and pays out a BIG win!
Note: Locking onto the central enemy will cause auto-target to fire at all the surrounding enemies first.
MEGA ENEMIES These extra large enemies are worth more than standard pays!
MEGA CYBORG RAIDER
MEGA RAMPAGING BEHEMOTH
THE FOUR BOSSES One of the 4 Bosses can spawn randomly at any time for a special battle. Every time they take damage, these bosses award a payout until
their HP reaches 10%. Once the Boss HP reaches 10% it will start to flash red and become ENRAGED. Once the boss is ENRAGED it will no
longer award any payouts until it is finally killed.
CAPSULES Frequent capsules will appear during every round of gameplay.
These capsules can contain a wide range of tools or prizes that can affect the game!

Each capsule can contain one of the following:
MONEY
CAPSULE Triggers a big win!
ARC
LIGHTNING Summons forks of lightning down on enemies, attacking and killing them!
MULTIPLIER
BOMB A bomb appears and kills several enemies, with potential for a multiplier! Doesn’t damage bosses.
FLASH
BLIZZARD All enemies are frozen in place for 10 seconds!
ENEMY

LASER

CHAIN REACTION
SHOT A bullet appears and kills several enemies! Doesn’t damage bosses.
TURRET Game rooms are sorted by the cost of each bullet. Select a cost per bullet value from the lobby to be taken to the first available room of that type.

Currency denomination and bet level do not change the chances of winning. It only changes the amount wagered and won.

The standard base weapon is the Turret, which can be aimed by using the onscreen crosshairwhich can be aimed by finger tap. The Turret has 5 bet levels.
This can be adjusted up by increasing your bet per shot. The shot payouts will get bigger as the Turret rises in level.
Making your bet larger by leveling the Turret only increases the amount wagered and the potential amount won.

Misuse/Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
CRITICAL HITS
Every shot has the potential to be a Critical Hit!
Critical Hits can affect any enemy except the 4 Bosses, Mega Enemies, and Capsules!
There is a random chance you will get a Critical Hit on an enemy, killing it instantly and awarding an
additional payout on top of the normal payout. This payout can receive a multiplier of 1x, 2x or 3x.
The bigger the enemy, the more likely to get a higher multiplier.
Mouse Symbol
Crosshair
The player’s cursor acts as a crosshair for their weapon. Any fired shot will hit wherever the cursor is pointed.
Keyboard
Commands
Spacebar: Fires the currently selected weapon wherever the cursor is pointing. Hold down to fire continuously.
Right Click
Locks Target
Click the crosshair button and then click an enemy to Lock On. It will then show a targeting reticule on the chosen enemy. Any shots fired by the player will hit this target, no matter what is in the way.
Left Click
Fires Weapon
Fires the current weapon at the location indicated by the crosshair. If no enemy target is present at the location of the crosshair, the bullet will bounce around until it hits an enemy target. Holding this button down allows for continuous fire until the button is released.
Buttons
Changes the bet level.
Sound
Turns all sounds off or on.
Back
Leaves the room and returns to the game lobby.
Settings
Opens the Settings Panel.
Note: Gameplay continues in background.
Finger Tap
