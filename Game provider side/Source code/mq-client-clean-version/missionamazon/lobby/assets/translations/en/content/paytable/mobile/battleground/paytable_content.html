HOW TO PLAY
ENTER THE BATTLEGROUNDS! Enter the Battlegrounds of MAX DUEL: MISSION: AMAZON! You have unlimited shots with the base, level 1 Turret. Enemies have a chance to drop POWERUPS for the Turret, increasing its bet level by 1, and awarding you shots per powerup. You can keep collecting powerups to a max level of 3.
EACH SHOT GUARANTEED TO HIT In the case that the fired shot does not strike your target, the turret shot will bounce off the edges of the screen until it hits another enemy. You can only have a maximum of active turret #shots:shot,shots# at any given time.
EACH SHOT HAS A CHANCE TO WIN Any shot fired has a chance to destroy the enemy it hits. Special Weapons have a higher chance to destroy enemies compared to the standard turret. Enemies can award cash payouts, turret powerups, Special Weapons, powered up Special Weapons or treasures for more prizes!

LEGENDARY BOSSES One or more of the Legendary Bosses have a chance to spawn after a random period of time, per round, initiating a special boss battle.
Special Weapons do additional damage to the Legendary Bosses. During the battle, the Legendary Boss will periodically award prizes as it takes damage.
A large prize is awarded when it is defeated. A 2x multiplier to the total payout from the Legendary Boss is awarded to the player who made the defeating shot.

WEAPONS The standard base weapon is the Turret, which can be aimed by using the onscreen crosshairwhich can be aimed by finger tap. The base level 1 weapon has infinite ammunition.

The bet level cannot be manually changed. Bet level powerups that last for ##MAX_SHOTS_PER_POWERUP## shots each can be earned through enemy drops, and powered up shots
can stack. Powerups are only valid for the base Turret weapon. There is no compensation for unused powerup shots at the end of the round. When
upgrading from level 2 to level 3, any unused level 2 shots are converted to level 3 shots. If you are already at the max level, any further powerups will
be converted into more shots at bet level 3.

Misuse/Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
TURRET The base weapon available at all times.
SPECIAL WEAPONS In addition to the base weapon, the Turret, Special Weapons can be won. All enemies may drop Special Weapons when
they are hit. These Special Weapons are stronger and can affect one or more enemies. Use the Special Weapons wisely: they are most effective when
used against strong enemies or groups of enemies. Special Weapon shots do not bounce off walls, but instead will target the nearest enemy to where you clicked tapped.
Weapon Carrier enemy can drop powered up versions of the Special Weapons that can be used.They offer a 2x, 3x, or 4x multiplier to damage.
PLASMA RIFLE Fires a stream of super-heated plasma that deals massive hits against up to ##SPECIAL_WEAPONS_MAX_TARGETS_COUNTS_1## enemies.
LASER Intense laser beam targets
up to ##SPECIAL_WEAPONS_MAX_TARGETS_COUNTS_0## enemies.
CRYOGUN Freezes enemies with a blast of super-chilled gas. Can freeze up to ##SPECIAL_WEAPONS_MAX_TARGETS_COUNTS_2## enemies.
FLAMETHROWER Engulfs an enemy in a stream of burning napalm.
Can hit up to ##SPECIAL_WEAPONS_MAX_TARGETS_COUNTS_3## enemies.
ARTILLERY STRIKE Smoke grenade calls down an artillery strike on its location, scoring enormous wins against up to ##SPECIAL_WEAPONS_MAX_TARGETS_COUNTS_4## enemies.
Weapons cannot be switched. Special Weapons are used as soon as they are won. If multiple Special Weapons are won, they will be used in
the order in which they were obtained. Unused Special Weapons are not compensated at the end of the round.
CRITICAL HITS
Special Weapons have the potential to deliver a Critical Hit to the enemy with every shot fired.

Critical Hits can affect any enemy except the Legendary Bosses!
Critical Hits apply a multiplier to the amount won from the shot fired.
Mouse Symbol
Crosshair
The players cursor acts as a crosshair for their weapon. Any fired shot will hit wherever the cursor is pointed.
Keyboard
Commands
Spacebar: Fires the currently selected weapon wherever the cursor is pointing. Hold down to fire continuously.
Left Click
Fires Weapon
Fires the current weapon at the location indicated by the crosshair. If no enemy target is present at the location of the crosshair, the bullet will bounce around until it hits an enemy target. Holding this button down allows for continuous fire until the button is released.
Right Click
Locks Target
Click the crosshair button and then click an enemy to Lock On. It will then show a targeting reticule on the chosen enemy. Any shots fired by the player will hit this target, no matter what is in the way.
Settings
Opens the Settings Panel.
Note: Gameplay continues in background.
Sound
Turns all sounds off or on.
Finger Tap
The player's finger tap position designates the intended target. Any fired shot will proceed to the location where the screen is tapped.
Fire Weapon
Fires the current weapon at the location indicated. If no enemy target is present at the tapped location, the bullet will bounce around until it hits an enemy target.

Holding their finger on the screen allows for continuous fire until the player stops touching the screen.
Lock Target
Tap the crosshair button and then tap an enemy to Lock On. It will then show a targeting reticule on the chosen enemy. Any shots fired by the player will hit this target, no matter what is in the way.
Settings
Opens the Settings Panel. Note: Gameplay continues in background.
Sound
Turns all sounds off or on.
