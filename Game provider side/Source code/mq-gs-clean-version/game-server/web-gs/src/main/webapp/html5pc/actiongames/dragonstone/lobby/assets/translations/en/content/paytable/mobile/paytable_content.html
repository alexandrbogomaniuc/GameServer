HOW TO PLAY
EACH SHOT GUARANTEED TO HIT In the case that the fired shot does not strike your target, the turret shot will bounce off the edges of the screen until it hits another enemy. You can only have a maximum of active turret #shots:shot,shots# at any given time. If any shots remain in the air at the end of the round without having hit any target, your wager will be returned to you. You cannot change your bet while you have active shots in flight.
EACH SHOT HAS A CHANCE TO WIN Any shot fired has a chance to destroy the enemy it hits. Special Weapons have a higher chance to destroy enemies compared to the standard turret. Enemies can award cash payouts, a spin on the in-game Mini-Slot bonus, fragments of the Legendary Dragon Stone, or Special Weapons!

WEAPONS Game rooms are sorted by the cost of each bullet. Select a cost per bullet value from the lobby to be taken to the first available room of that type.

Currency denomination and bet level do not change the chances of winning. It only changes the amount wagered and won.

The standard base weapon is the Turret, which can be aimed by using the onscreen crosshairwhich can be aimed by finger tap. The Turret has 5 bet levels.
This can be adjusted up by increasing your bet per shot. The shot payouts will get bigger as the Turret rises in level.
Making your bet larger by leveling the Turret only increases the amount wagered and the potential amount won.

Misuse/Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
TURRET The base weapon available at all times.
SPECIAL WEAPONS In addition to the base weapon, the Turret, all enemies except for the Dragon may drop Special Weapons when they are killed. These Special Weapons are stronger
and can affect one or more enemies. Use the Special Weapons wisely: they are most effective when used against strong enemies or groups of enemies.
Special Weapons that are dropped by enemies do not require a wager to fire. Special Weapon shots do not bounce off walls, but instead will target the nearest
enemy to where you clicked tapped. Unused special weapons get converted into cash prizes at the end of the round.

You can purchase a pay-per-shot Special Weapon at any time during gameplay! These shots multiply your wager depending on the chosen weapon. Simply click tap on the image of the Special Weapon you want to use on the left side of the screen. Free shots from dropped Special Weapons are played at the bet level they were won at, and the bet level cannot be changed until the free shots are used. Special weapons must be used in the round they were won in. Any unused special weapons are lost at the end of the round.
CRITICAL HITS
Special Weapons have the potential to deliver a Critical Hit to the enemy with every shot fired.

Critical Hits can affect any enemy except the Dragon and the Specter Enemies!
Critical Hits apply a multiplier to the amount won from the shot fired.
Mouse Symbol
Crosshair
The players cursor acts as a crosshair for their weapon. Any fired shot will hit wherever the cursor is pointed.
Keyboard
Commands
Spacebar: Fires the currently selected weapon wherever the cursor is pointing. Hold down to fire continuously.
Right Click
Locks Target
Click the crosshair button and then click an enemy to Lock On. It will then show a targeting reticule on the chosen enemy. Any shots fired by the player will hit this target, no matter what is in the way.
Left Click
Fires Weapon
Fires the current weapon at the location indicated by the crosshair. If no enemy target is present at the location of the crosshair, the bullet will bounce around until it hits an enemy target. Holding this button down allows for continuous fire until the button is released.
Special
Weapons Use any Special Weapon at any time on demand by clicking the desired weapons button on the left side of the screen. The Cost Per Shot of the weapon is indicated on the button.
Finger Tap
The players finger tap position designates the intended target. Any fired shot will proceed to the location where the screen is tapped.
Lock Target
Tap the crosshair button and then tap an enemy to Lock On. It will then show a targeting reticule on the chosen enemy. Any shots fired by the player will hit this target, no matter what is in the way.
Fire Weapon
Fires the current weapon at the location indicated. If no enemy target is present at the tapped location, the bullet will bounce around until it hits an enemy target.

Holding their finger on the screen allows for continuous fire until the player stops touching the screen.
Special
Weapons Use any Special Weapon at any time on demand by tapping the desired weapons button on the left side of the screen. The Cost Per Shot of the weapon is indicated on the button.
